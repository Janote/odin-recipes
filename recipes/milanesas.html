Milanesas

Milanesas, Classic.

This is my favorite meal, look at this one, it is just... Perfect.

If you ask me, I would add Ketchup.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Chopped Parsley
  • Salt and pepper
  • Bread crumbs
  • 1 kilo of buttock

Steps

  1. Peel the garlic and chop it together with the parsley.
  2. Place the eggs in a bowl and beat them until well dissolved. Then add the parsley, garlic, and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Place the meat in the mixture from the previous step, let it sit for a few minutes, remove, and coat with breadcrumbs.
  4. In a pan with plenty of hot oil, fry the breaded meat. Remove and drain on paper towels.
