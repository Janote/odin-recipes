Beef Empanadas

Ingredients

  • 500 grams minced beef
  • 1/2 kilogram chopped onions
  • 2 chopped green onions
  • 1 clove chopped garlic
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch (Maizena)
  • 1 can of tomatoes
  • Oil (as needed)
  • Dried ground chili (as needed)
  • Dried oregano (as needed)
  • Ground cumin (as needed)
  • Ground paprika (as needed)
  • 12 empanada dough discs
  • 1 egg for brushing

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, sauté the onion, green onions, and garlic.
  2. Once the vegetables are well-cooked, add the minced beef and the canned tomatoes. Cook for approximately 10 minutes.
  3. Add the dissolved Maizena® in cold water and cook for an additional 1 minute. Season with spices and add the hard-boiled egg. Reserve and let it cool.
  4. Assemble the empanadas, seal the edges, and brush with beaten egg.
  5. Bake in an oven at 200°C until they are golden brown.
